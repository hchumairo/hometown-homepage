Top three places to go at Bandung

Kawah Putih

Kawah Putih is a beautiful lake with turquoise water and white sulfur deposits. It is known for its cool air, tea plantations, and hot springs.

Saung Angklung Udjo

Saung Angklung Udjo features bamboo crafts, musical instruments, and performances of traditional Sundanese music and dance.

Tebing Keraton

Tebing Keraton is a great place to relax. Visitors can do many things, such as enjoying the view, taking photos, camping, tracking, and hiking.
